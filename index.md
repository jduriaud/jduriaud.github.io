### Physics & Numerical Methods

[Quantum wave packets and tunnelling in one dimension](https://github.com/jduriaud/schrodinger-tdse)

<p align="center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/jduriaud/schrodinger-tdse/main/figures/tunnel.gif" style="max-height:320px; max-width:100%;" alt="A wave packet tunnelling through a potential barrier, shown as a space-time density plot"/></p>

Numerical solution of the 1D time-dependent Schrödinger equation: a wave
packet travelling, reflecting off a potential, and tunnelling through a
barrier, compared using an explicit scheme and Crank-Nicolson.

[A molecular-dynamics simulation of a two-dimensional gas](https://github.com/jduriaud/lennard-jones-gas)

<p align="center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/jduriaud/lennard-jones-gas/main/figures/gas.gif" style="max-height:320px; max-width:100%;" alt="A 2D gas of Lennard-Jones particles, coloured by speed"/></p>

A gas of interacting particles simulated from Newton's equations under the
Lennard-Jones force, recovering the Maxwell-Boltzmann speed distribution and
the equation of state from the microscopic trajectory.

[Force distribution and natural modes of a truss bridge](https://github.com/jduriaud/truss-bridge)

<p align="center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/jduriaud/truss-bridge/main/figures/vehicle.gif" style="max-height:320px; max-width:100%;" alt="A vehicle crossing a truss bridge, with bars coloured by axial force"/></p>

A plane truss bridge analysed with the direct stiffness method: force
distribution under load, a vehicle-crossing simulation, and the structure's
natural modes of vibration.

[Dust filtering by a planet gap in a protoplanetary disk](https://github.com/jduriaud/protoplanetary-gap-dust-filtering)

<p align="center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/jduriaud/protoplanetary-gap-dust-filtering/main/figures/disk_evolution.gif" style="max-height:320px; max-width:100%;" alt="Gas and dust surface-density maps of a protoplanetary disk as a planet opens a gap and a dust ring forms at its outer edge"/></p>

A 2D multifluid hydrodynamic study, built on FARGO3D, of how a planet opens a
gap in its protoplanetary disk and filters the dust drifting through it:
gas and dust surface densities, the pressure maximum at the gap edge, and the
size-dependent depletion of grains across two parameter series.
